Hello! We Are Gajare Photography Production House

I am Sachin Gajare, a Professional Photographer and Founder/CEO of Gajare Photography. I started shooting pictures and videos long ago with just a smartphone. To carry out my skills further I joined and started assisting some well-known and big photographers in the industry. Later I joined Mumbai Photo Academy (Borivali) for certification and now here I am. Call us for any photoshoot events like Engagement, Pre-wedding, Wedding, Maternity, Baby shower, Birthday shoot, Product Photography, or Food Photography.
